Welcome to Investify

Investify is a stock market simulation game where you can learn to trade with virtual money. You start with $10,000 to invest in real stocks using live market data.

Example:

Think of this as a safe sandbox to practice before real trading.

Key Tips:

  • This is completely virtual money - no real money at risk
  • All stock prices are real and updated live
  • You can compete with friends and climb leaderboards

Understanding Stocks

Stocks represent ownership shares in a company. When you buy a stock, you own a tiny piece of that company. Stock prices go up and down based on company performance and market conditions.

Example:

If you buy Apple (AAPL) at $150 and sell at $160, you profit $10 per share.

Key Tips:

  • Stock prices change throughout trading hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET)
  • Price movements reflect investor sentiment and company news
  • You make money when you sell stocks for more than you paid

How to Trade Stocks

Trading is simple! Go to the Market page, search for any stock, and click on it to open the trade modal. You can buy or sell shares with just a few clicks.

How to Buy:

1. Go to Market → Search for a stock → Click on it → Select "Buy" → Choose shares → Click "Execute Trade"

How to Sell:

1. Go to Market → Click "My Holdings" → Click "Trade" on any stock → Select "Sell" → Choose shares → Click "Execute Trade"

Key Tips:

  • You can search by company name (Apple) or symbol (AAPL)
  • Use the "Max" button to buy/sell all available shares
  • Check your cash balance before buying
  • View your holdings in the "My Holdings" section

Profit, Loss & Risk

Your profit or loss is the difference between what you paid and the current stock price. Higher potential returns usually come with higher risk of losses.

Example:

Buy 10 shares at $100 each = $1,000 invested. If price goes to $110, you have $100 profit.

Key Tips:

  • Green numbers = profit, Red numbers = loss
  • Don't panic over short-term price movements
  • Diversify your portfolio to reduce risk

Building Your Portfolio

A portfolio is your collection of different stocks. Diversification means investing in different companies and industries to spread risk.

Example:

Instead of buying only tech stocks, also consider healthcare, retail, or energy companies.

Key Tips:

  • Don't put all money in one stock
  • Mix different industries (tech, healthcare, finance)
  • Monitor your portfolio's overall performance

Competition & Friends

Compete with friends by sharing your friend code. Climb the leaderboards and see who can achieve the highest returns. Weekly competitions offer special rewards.

Example:

Your friend code is shown in your profile - share it to compete with friends

Key Tips:

  • Share your friend code to add trading buddies
  • Check leaderboards to see your ranking
  • Participate in weekly competitions for extra rewards

Achievements & Rewards

Earn achievements by reaching trading milestones, maintaining streaks, and achieving profit targets. Each achievement gives you experience points and bragging rights.

Example:

Double your starting money ($10k → $20k) to earn the 'Doubled Money' achievement

Key Tips:

  • Complete your first trade to earn 'First Trade' badge
  • Maintain daily trading streaks for bonus rewards
  • Achievements unlock as you progress

Safety & Education

Remember, this is educational and uses virtual money only. Take advantage of the 'Did You Know?' tips and use this experience to learn about real investing.

Example:

Learn concepts like diversification, risk tolerance, and market volatility in a safe environment

Key Tips:

  • All money in Investify is virtual and educational
  • Read the educational tips that appear while trading
  • Use this knowledge to understand real investing principles
